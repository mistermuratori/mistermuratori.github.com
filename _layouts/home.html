Life doesn’t need to be complicated; a simple clean normal size bike which happens to can be a small package; no cables, no gears, no paint. And besides using your regular bank, you can also pay for it with bitcoin or your other favourite cryptocurrency Designed following old school traditions by the swiss based brand of Mistermuratori, the bike frame is 100% made in holland using cutting edge technologies. The bike has a stainless steel frame, so it will never rust and needs no paint, no gears, just a simple and trusted coasterbrake. Foldable with just one lever, it simply fits in the booth of your car, the train, a plane or your metropolis appartment..
